**I looked sadness in the eyes** **And I smiled** Even broken by the life
I chose **From the window I watched every season slip away**

Like the trees, I stay in the same place In autumn, in winter, I wait
for spring to arrive

**From the road I wanted to go back to where I started** **From the distance I caught sight of
joy and hope** From the crumbs we waste, we will make dinner **I
would go back** **To try to warn myself**

**That the path will be dark** **But that Christ is the light of the world** Let Him
tell you who you are **Let the Word undo you** **Let it drown you in His
grace** Only the cross will hide who you are not

I looked sadness in the eyes And smiled
