Seriously, the party is because of **this**? Is **this** the goal? Is it in **this way** that we will "make" the difference? Is this why we are here? Is this the means by which we await the end?

You know what I'm talking about, I know what I'm talking about.

I cannot judge, but I have **the obligation to discern**. And we already know this story, this mixture, this salad, this disgusting gospel, and where we're going.

Governed by Bishops, Apostles, Kings, or whatever else… we are a nation governed by wolves dressed up as shepherds, driven by destructive arrogance and pride.

We see people clinging to their _images of Christ_, their _visions_, their _projects_, their _impostle-doctrines_, and their **religious lies** that drive EVERYONE away from Christ's gospel.

I grew up watching this, since the 80s and the _denominations_, through the 80s and the _new birth_, the 90s and their _communities_, the end of the 90s and the birth of the _Korean cells_, the "**how**" to **move** the Holy Spirit, and the abortion of the infamous **G12**, which exploded into various micro-visions that have "_nothing_" to do with G12, but whose own leaders admit are "the _same thing_" with different names.

There are also the false-churches that promise enrichment in exchange for donations…

And now, when it seems there's no way left to descend, we have an invasion and an exponential growth of the **_Gospel market_**… _marketing_ pastors, _politicians_, _congressmen_, _millionaires_, owners of "NGOs," and so on… and more and more we see a "more evangelical Brazil"… MILLIONS OF EVANGELICALS and the country is still the same crap!

You, me, every Christian needs to question themselves: **_If there are more evangelicals in your office, why isn't there more transparency, honesty, and love for neighbor_**?

If **_there are more evangelicals in your city, why does crime keep increasing, poverty grows, and traffic continues with thousands of fights_**?

We get anxious waiting for the new CD of last week's most famous band, we pack concerts, **block access to what is "of the world"**, sell "**_our_**" anointed _politicians_, anointed _socks_, anointed _temples_, and even gospel _demonstrations_… we are so different, so different from the world, that if we put everyone living on the same planet, we wouldn't make THE SLIGHTEST DIFFERENCE!

I'm tired of seeing quotes like:

  * Everyone knows that HE has these flaws, but we have to obey him, he is the chosen one, these flaws don't change the fact that he is THE GUY; (when referring to the church pastor, or an "untouchable" leader)
OR:

  * Ah, so-and-so has a character flaw, he (_pick a sin: cheats on his wife, evades taxes, hits his kids, lies_), and that's why he will NEVER have what God has for him; (when referring to a person who has nothing to offer)

I have already seen so many disgusting things, so many wounds with pus, that **I decided** some time ago **to never again keep silent**.

I have 100 friends on Facebook, 50% or more must be Christian… if what I'm saying you don't see at your church, or at the neighboring church, no problem, this text isn't for you.

Now if you do see this in your church, if your church is a stage for politicians, or false prophets, I encourage you: **Do not bend your knees to this attitude**. **Do not accept** this kind of gospel. **Fight** for the kingdom, **fight** for the principles, **fight** for Christianity.

**The Gospel of the evangelicals turns my stomach!**
