Today's "praise" is by **_Djavan – Um amor Puro_**.

Love God above all things, give Him all your time, all your hours.
Worship Him in everything you do, love Him above all else and you can be
sure that no matter the situation, ugly or beautiful, if He is with you,
there will always be a blue sky in the end. _(**Matthew 8:26**)_

What is inside my heart I have kept to give to you.  
And all the hours that time has to grant me, are yours until I die.

I worship you in everything.  
I want more than anything to love You without limits.  
To live a great story.

**Here or somewhere else, which can be ugly or beautiful**  
**If we are together there will be a blue sky.**
