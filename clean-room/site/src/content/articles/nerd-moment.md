Nerd Moment:

A flea lives on average 3 months
A human being lives on average 80 years, give or take 960 months.

That means we live 320 * longer than a flea.

With that in mind. 1 human minute, in the life of a flea, equals approximately 30 flea minutes.

[Joana](https://www.facebook.com/joana.fiorentini.brito) just threw a flea out the window of the 15th floor, and by my calculations of weight, air resistance, and distance, it took about 20 seconds to hit the ground. 1/3 of a minute.

We can easily say this flea was flying until it hit the ground for the equivalent of 10 human minutes.

What a radical little flea!!!
