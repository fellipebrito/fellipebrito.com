If you are discriminated against for being an alcoholic, I am an alcoholic with you;
If you are discriminated against for being poor, I am poor with you;
If you are discriminated against for being an atheist, I am an atheist with you;
If you are discriminated against for being a Christian, I am a Christian with you;
If you are discriminated against for being a foreigner, I am a foreigner with you;
If you are discriminated against for being gay, I am gay with you;
If you are discriminated against for being black, and called a monkey, I am a monkey and black with you;
And I do this because of the Gospel, to also be a participant in it;
because if I am discriminated against for being human and a sinner, my God becomes human and the greatest of sinners with me.

1 Corinthians 9
