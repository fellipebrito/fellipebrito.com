Today's "praise" is by **_Arrais – Montreal_**.

ISIS made new victims in the city of light. A day before, however, the same
group killed more than 40 people and wounded another 200 in an attack in
Beirut. Soon it will be Iraqi and Syrian families who will have their lives
shattered by the counterattack to come – it always comes –, of the
international military forces.

Our solidarity, as selective as it is fleeting, prevents us from seeing what
happens below the equator line. Even with the absurd things that happen in
Brazil we hardly write testimonies or change avatars in solidarity with the
families.

Unfortunately, we don't expect anything good in the coming days, because ISIS,
more than a terrorist group, is an ideology that resonates deeply in the heart
of part of those who "don't matter." And its siren song, of power and
vengeance, has won over more and more followers. I am very sorry for the
Parisian, Lebanese, Syrian and Iraqi families who are victims of this war. And
I am sorry for us who, by closing our eyes to what is different, do not see
what makes us equal and human.

The only sane way I find to be able to face this world is the hope that there
is something more. We live in a lost world… today my heart is broken and
without hope, this song is on loop on my radio.

I want to breathe the air of a new home and catch sight of other hills. That
would conspire against what I have already seen of the known landscapes.

Having the grass as my bed and the sky as ceiling, to forget my wounds and the
memories of this life I will plant and water with new beginnings.

I close my eyes and fill my chest with the air of a hill Where rich and poor
will eat from the king's table.
