More than 2 years ago I wrote about all of this that is happening today.

The lack of respect is on both sides, except only one side has as a
"commandment" to love your neighbor as yourself. (Matthew 22:39) The lack of
care is on both sides, but only one side had as its founder someone who
preferred to die without defending himself rather than accept the help of a
disciple's sword. (John 18:10)

Do you want to know what it is to "offend" the son of God? It is to arrest him
in the dead of night (Matthew 26:4), judge him (Matthew 27:11), beat and spit on
him, and then nail him semi-naked to a cross until death. That is offense, yes.
And do you know what his response was?

"Forgive them, they don't know what they are doing." (Luke 23:34)

It scares me to see my Christian friends demanding a response and a union
against all of this. This is anti-biblical, anti-Christian, anti-everything God
taught us.

When everyone gathered to "justify the family" and arrest a prostitute who was
sleeping with a married man, and do exactly as was written in the Bible of the
time, Jesus changed the rules, and the story ends with a "Neither do I condemn
you" (John 8:10,11).

Here's a tip: leave your condemnation at home, in front of the mirror, and at
the next gay parade, grab your dishes, go there, offer free food, "wash their
feet" and stop fighting… sin is abounding (Romans 5:20,21), but love covers a
multitude of sins. (1 Peter 4:8)

I quote here a part of what I wrote years ago, if you want to read it all,
click the link:  
"We are feeding in ourselves this revanchism, this hatred, that brings nothing
to anyone. Evangelicals post, talk, share the 'war against depraved
homosexuals,' and on the other side we have those who fight strongly against
the evangelicals, their rites, practices and beliefs.

In the long run, all of this is VERY dangerous. We see small signs of a civil
war, and all of this should be much more concerning than who I, you or our
neighbor sleeps with.

We are all on the same ground. Most point to fences and walls… I choose THE
way."
