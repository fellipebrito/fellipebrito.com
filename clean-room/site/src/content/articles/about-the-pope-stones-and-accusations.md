An evangelical pastor, famous in the city where I lived, just posted on Twitter: _Stop admiring the Pope!!! Nothing has changed, the Pope continues to be a Pagan Idolater!!!_

He also speaks about how it "hurts his heart" the idolatrous nation we have (all this from seeing the Pope kiss an image).

Well, dear pastor, know that **our nation is indeed Catholic**, and **unfortunately** has been **becoming** more and more _evangelical_, _less Christian_, and much more idolatrous.

**We exchanged** our **images** for "**mega preachers and gospel singers**," our **amulets** for "**anointed objects**," our **patuás** (folk charms) for "**oil** of anointing brought **from Israel**," and even the way we distribute "**passe**" (a spiritualist blessing) for catchy and lying phrases like "**I am releasing** the anointing."

We are **more and more evangelical** and **every day less just**. We are **more and more evangelical** and **every day more murderous**, **thieving**, **manipulative**, **corrupt**.

Sorry to disappoint you, dear pastor, but **your church does not carry the truth**. You don't have a permit to be the only house to worship God, much less the last portal to heaven.

The **evangelical church**, as it is called in Brazil's hinterlands, **is the greatest cancer** of our nation. We sell a **canned Jesus**, **manipulative**, **selfish**, **stone-throwing**, owner of the truth, and **miracle-working**.

**_We ask of the people 21-day fasts_**, but we forget that **what God wants is care for widows and orphans.**

We "use" the _Holy Spirit_ in congresses and trips around the country to "preach the word," but we don't pray for our _sick neighbor, for our child's friend at school_.

**We preach to thousands** through the internet daily, but we mercilessly fail **at the human salvation of our neighborhood**.

**We want to win the world for Christ**, **but not even we ourselves are His**. We don't act with **grace**, **love**, **mercy**.

We don't sit at a table if there is someone smoking, we don't go to a bar on a friend's birthday because it isn't a holy place.

_**Jesus was not like that.**_
I see in this pastor the same **blind** and **petty** attitude of **many** homosexuals.

Some hope the Pope will go totally crazy and say **_that being homosexual is "Catholic."_**

Others hope the Pope will go totally crazy and say **_that any image of a saint is from the devil._**

It is so much pettiness, a fight to be the "owner of the **TRUTH**," a lack of **CONSISTENCY** in the preaching, a **DIFFERENCE** in **WEIGHTS** and measures of judgment…

ON CONSISTENCY:
These same people who accuse the Pope say they respect and love Catholics, homosexuals, but at times they lack respect for the most "sacred" things to them. But woe, woe to whoever speaks ill of a bunch of people speaking in tongues without understanding each other inside a meeting after midnight.

ON DIFFERENT WEIGHTS
These same people who accuse the Pope let Sonia, Hernanes, Malafaia, Feliciano, René TerraNova go unpunished…

ON TRUTH
We have a choice: Do we want to be ruled by the Stone or by Grace?

  * If by the Stone, we will stone each other for our sins, and the last one stones himself;
  * If by grace, we will hug each other. I embrace your flaws, you embrace mine, and together we seek in God a better humanity.

May God enlighten us and have mercy on us.
