Today's "praise" is by **Arrais - Fogo**.

I am shipwrecked on a land where greed is law.

I hear the cry of anguish of an enslaved people here, they don't know they are
trapped. They go on with life like that.

I set fire to what's left of my boat, so I won't be tempted to abandon the
mission.
