"Living in the United States is good, but it sucks. Living in Brazil sucks, but it's good." – Tom Jobim

This one is dedicated to my friends who think I'm a playboy with life made, to those who want to move here at all costs and "suffer" for living in Brazil, and also to myself, who sometimes feels like running back…

Carpe diem* my friend!

* Carpe diem is a Latin phrase from a poem by Horace, popularly translated as "seize the day" or "enjoy the moment."
