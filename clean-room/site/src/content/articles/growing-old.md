How do we say goodbye to what we had? The good times that made us laugh
are far greater than the bad ones.

I thought all of that would be forever, but "forever" went away. It is so
hard to say "goodbye" to yesterday.

I have no idea where this road I took is going to lead me. All I
know is where we have been and what we faced.

If we are going to make it to tomorrow, I hope it will be worth the wait. It is
so hard to say "goodbye" to yesterday.

I will always carry the memories with me, so they can be the sun after the rain. It
is so hard to say "goodbye" to yesterday.
