If for you:

  * Zeca Pagodinho saying "_I've been through almost everything in this life and now I'm waiting for my turn. Let life take me_" is the Brazilian people living the Brazilian "jeitinho," doing things halfway, being the worst of what the Brazilian has to offer

  * but the "boys from Liverpool" saying "_Whenever I'm in trouble, I remember Mother Mary speaking words of wisdom: 'let it be_'" is the wisdom, the poetry, and the deep lyric of musical geniuses.

You are a "mongrel*."
