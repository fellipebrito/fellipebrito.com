I have an idea for Churches that want to "fight homosexuality."

How about investing time in protecting marriages. Investing time in encouraging
men to be such good fathers and passionate husbands that their daughters
dream of "having" a man like them.

How about investing quality time so that couples understand each other, speak
the same language, and so the kids can look and want a marriage like
theirs.

The current "brilliant idea" of the church is to come down hard on
homosexuality, forgetting the high rate of adultery and separations that
happen inside the church itself. Forgetting the high number of girls who say:
"– A husband like my dad?!? Never!"

Maybe that way, when we can offer something more interesting, we won't even
need to point fingers here or there. Maybe being the difference we want to see in
the world is already enough to see a different world.

My idea is just a copy of the master's idea, who said: Before you
look at the splash of tomato sauce on your friend's shirt you should look at
those clothes of yours all muddy with sin.
