Lately, when people ask me what my favorite gospel band is, I answer without thinking: U2!

The funny thing is, I don't listen to them on loop, and I don't even know their whole discography (that's changing). But they're the ones I like the most. The lyrics, the mission, the purpose… yes, they're my favorite band. Here's one of the examples of why:

_You're packing a suitcase for a place none of us has been._ _A place that has to be believed to be seen._ _You could've flown away, far away…_ _a singing bird in an open cage_

_You've got to leave it behind:_ _All that you fashion_ _All that you make_ _All that you build_ _All that you break_ _All that you measure_ _All that you feel_ _All that you sense_ _All that you scheme_ _All that you dress up_ _All that you see_ _All that you create_ _All that you destroy_ _All that you hate_ _All that you reason, it's only time_ _All that, you can leave behind_

_Love is not the easiest thing, and of all the baggage you can bring, it's all that you can't leave behind._

_And if the darkness is to keep us apart, or if the daylight feels like it's a long way off;_ _And if your glass heart should crack, or for a second you turn back;_ _Oh, no! Be strong!!!_

_Walk on! Walk on!_ _What you got they can't steal! They can't even feel it!_ _What you've fought for they can't deny, can't sell, can't buy!_ _Walk on! Walk on! Stay safe tonight_

_And I know it aches, and your heart it breaks_ _You can only take so much, but I'm telling you:_ _Walk on! Walk on!_
